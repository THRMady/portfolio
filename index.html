Here's some of our most recent projects

MindMum

A PDeC App for mums' wellbeing

MindMum App is designed to assist expectant and new mothers with the emotional challenges that this time of life often brings. It provides effective strategies to help you lift your mood, strengthen your relationships and feel supported and confident in becoming a mum.

App Store

Fitz

A chatbot to help you change

Fitz is like a coach on your phone, which uses mental imagery to help you build motivation to reach your goals and keep going when things get tough. You can talk about your plans, see your progress, and use photos and audios to help you create powerful images.

Google Play
App Store

niggle

Your wellbeing toolkit by Kids Helpline

The Niggle app tracks young people’s physical and mental health and their issues (niggles). The app provides personalized resources (tip sheets, apps, videos, podcasts, others’ stories) and activities that can help them to manager their niggles.

Google Play
App Store

INSIST

An app for suicide prevention

INSIST app supports INSIST trained responders (Indigenous Network Suicide Intervention Skills Training) with managing people at-risk of suicide. Users can search a directory of local support services, connect with responders’ network, revise training over time and more.

Google Play
App Store

QuikFix

A platform for clinical intervention

QuikFix app is a clinical companion web app to deliver a substance-use intervention to young people over the phone or in-person. It is based on brief motivational interviewing and personality-targeted interventions. The app also has a trial management component with research manager and research assistant interfaces.
